---
name: Chicken Soup
---

## Ingredients

 - 3 chicken thighs
 - 32 fluid ounces of Swanson chicken stock

   <details>
   <summary>Note</summary>

   > It should be Swanson, since that brand provides the best flavor based on our experience.
   
   </details>

 - 2 cups water
 - 2 tbsp garlic (you can use "jarlic")
 - 1 large onion, diced (or 2 small onions)
 - 1/2 bag of carrots
 - poultry seasoning
 - Celery (small bag)
 - Salt and pepper to taste
 - 1 1/2 cups noodles

## Directions

 1. Preheat crockpot
 2. Brown chicken, adding salt and pepper. When you flip the chicken, add more salt and pepper to the other side.
 3. Remove chicken from pan and add onions, carrots and celery.
 4. Add garlic, poultry seasoning, 2 cups water. Scrape the bottom of the pan to get all the yummy bits (this will also make cleaning easier!)
 5. Once the mixture is simmering, add to the crock pot
 6. Add the chicken to the crockpot, along with all the juice.
 7. Add 2 bay leaves and chicken stock.
 8. 30 minutes before servicing, separate the chicken with a fork and add noodles.
